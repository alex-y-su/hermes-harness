# Hermes Harness Domain Brief

Hermes Harness is a Hermes-first orchestration system for durable AI agent teams. The main team keeps strategy, priorities, context, and accountability on a Kanban board, then delegates execution to hosted remote sub-teams.

The product is built around remote Kanban teams: long-lived Hermes agents with their own boards, memory, working context, schedules, and reporting loops. Main-team cards assigned to `team:<name>` are routed to the registered remote team instead of being executed by a local profile.

Remote teams are expected to own execution. They choose strategy and cadence, perform the work, keep KPI checks current, maintain schedules while a card is running, and report blockers or outcomes back to the originating main-team card.

The key audience is AI builders, founder-operators, and teams building autonomous agent operations. The strongest content themes are accountable agent delegation, remote sub-team ownership, Kanban-based coordination, durable execution, KPI reporting, stop/continue rules, and replacing one-shot prompts with operating loops.

For X/social content, posts should make the value concrete: a main team can delegate a high-level business outcome to a specialist sub-team, keep receipts and decisions in Kanban, continue work through a maintenance loop, and receive structured reports with evidence instead of losing context in chat.
