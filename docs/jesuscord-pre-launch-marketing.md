# Jesuscord Pre-Product Marketing Plan

**Context:** Roomcord exists as a general-purpose AI messenger. The Jesuscord mod (Christian-specific theme, templates, content, hubs) is **not yet shipped**. North-star goal remains 10M installs in 90 days post-launch.

**Mental shift:** Stop thinking "marketing before product." Start thinking **"manufacturing the launch-day proof."** Every pre-launch action should answer: *what specific, quotable thing will this let us say on launch day?*

The trap with pre-product marketing is burning the one launch window on hype that doesn't convert. The goal isn't "make noise now" — it's **build distribution rails and proof we can credibly use when the mod ships.**

---

## Plays, ranked by leverage

### 1. Founding Pastors program (highest leverage)

Recruit 50–200 pastors as **design partners**, not customers.

- **They get:** free forever tier, naming credit, direct line to founder, monthly call.
- **They give:** feedback, a launch-day post to their congregation, a pre-claimed hub.
- **Math:** 200 pastors × 1,000 congregants × 30% activation = ~60K real users on day one. Not 10M, but a credible base camp.
- **Playbook lineage:** Superhuman / Notion early access.
- **Risk:** 4–8 weeks of manual outreach. No shortcut.

### 2. Run real prayer circles / small groups on vanilla Roomcord

Don't wait for the mod. Host sessions now under a "Jesuscord beta" brand using whatever Roomcord features exist.

- Each session = testimonial, clip, case study.
- Manufacturing the proof needed later: *"500 prayer circles hosted, 12K participants, 87% return weekly."*
- Side benefit: de-risks the mod spec — learn what templates actually work before building them.

### 3. Sermon-clipping content engine (start immediately)

The `sermons` team spec already exists in archived docs. Operationalize it now without the mod.

- Clip sermons from 10–20 willing pastors.
- Post to TikTok / Shorts / Reels under a Jesuscord brand.
- CTA: "Full sermon at [pastor's link]" → evolves to "join the waitlist" → "open the app" as launch nears.
- **Target:** 100K cumulative views before launch. Achievable in 60 days with consistent posting.
- Pastors get free distribution; we get a content moat and an audience.

### 4. "Claim your hub" waitlist (not a generic email signup)

Skip the boring email capture. Build a page where pastors enter their church name and **pre-claim their Jesuscord hub URL**.

- Psychological commitment is ~10× a generic signup.
- Captures real segmentation data: church name, size, location, denomination.
- **Target:** 1,000 pre-claimed hubs before mod ships.

### 5. Build-in-public on X via @roomcordhq

Founder narrative: *"We're building Jesuscord. Here's what we learned this week from Pastor X."*

- Weekly cadence, specific, no platitudes.
- Slow-burn — won't deliver users directly, but builds the story journalists and podcasts pick up later.
- Pair with 3–5 podcast appearances on Christian/pastor-tech podcasts in the launch month.

---

## What NOT to do pre-product

- **No paid acquisition.** Can't retain what doesn't exist. Burns cash for vanity.
- **No big PR push.** Only one launch window — save it for mod-ship + 100 testimonials.
- **No generic "Christian community coming!" landing page.** Indistinguishable from 50 dead startups. Specificity (Founding Pastors, claim-your-hub, real clips) is the differentiator.
- **No promising features that don't exist.** Pastors have long memories and small networks talk.

---

## Suggested 90-day sequence

| Phase | Days | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| Phase 1 | 1–30 | Recruit 20 Founding Pastors. Start clipping sermons from 5 of them. Stand up claim-your-hub page. Begin build-in-public weekly cadence. |
| Phase 2 | 30–60 | Run 20+ live prayer circles / small groups on vanilla Roomcord. Capture testimonials + clips. Scale to 50–100 Founding Pastors. First podcast appearances. |
| Phase 3 | 60–90 | Mod ships to Founding Pastors only (closed beta). Their congregations onboard. Public launch is **their testimonials, not our promises**. |

---

## Success metrics by launch day

- 100+ Founding Pastors signed
- 1,000+ pre-claimed hubs
- 500+ live prayer circles / small groups hosted on vanilla Roomcord
- 100K+ cumulative short-form video views
- 20+ pastor testimonial clips ready
- 3–5 podcast appearances aired
